Metals usually posses diferrent crystal structures. Each crystal structure have different arrangement of atoms in it. The most common seven crystal structures are as follows

  1. Cubic
  2. tetragonal
  3. Orthorhombic
  4. Trigonal
  5. Hexagonal
  6. Monoclinic
  7. triclinic

BCC

The BCC crystal structure belongs to the cubic family, All axis are perpendicular to eachoher and have same length. There are two atoms per unit cell, One at the centre and one is at the corners.There are eight corners per unit cell. Each corner contains one atom, which is being shared in eight unit cells.
